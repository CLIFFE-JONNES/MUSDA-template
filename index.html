    Study

    Prayer and Fellowship

    The prayer band keeps the prayer spirit of the church. The morning glory is also employed every weekday in the same. We also meet for vespers services on Wednesday and Friday evening.

    Evangelism

    This is achieved through end-semester missions, literature evangelism, door-to-door visitations and medical missionary. The social media tools like whatsapp, wordpress, etc are also employed in the same.

    About MUSDA

    Maseno University Seventh Day Adventist Church - MUSDA, is part of the global Seventh Day Adventist church, organized under the Public Campus Ministries, Kenya. It is primarily composed of Students dedicated in Glorifying God by being true to their name- Seventh Day Adventists, and serving humanity through their courses.

    Our Mission

    By the comission of Matthew 28:18-11, our mission is to take the Advent message to all the world in this generation. Our mission to the world is not to serve or please ourselves; we are to glorify God by co-operating with Him to save sinners.
    COL 142.3

    Activities

    We have various services as a church. These include the following:

    Vespers services

    We have two vespers services weekly. These are Wednesday vespers(6.45pm-8.00pm) and Friday Vespers(6.45pm-9.00pm).

    Sabbath Services

    Our Sabbath services begin at 8.00am Sabbath, and end at 5.00pm. We also have a Bible study after the Sabbath from 6.45pm-9.00pm.

    Mission

    We have missions at the end of every semester. This is an evangelism open air program that helps propagate the gospel to the general public.

    Internal Crusade

    We also have an inreach program that helps us in spiritual nourishment. This equipes our members to live and preach the gospel to all in the course of the semester in preparation for the end-semester outreach mission

    Medical Missionary

    The health message is the right arm of the three angel's message, thus an opening wedge for the gospel.

    Ministries and Music groups

    There is a sweet influence and saving power in good music. We have the church choir, Philadelphia, Sweet Spirit, amidst other groups that help in ministry and music.
